A) Demand Momentum (Claims)

Adoption Inflection

Concentration Risk

Top Systems Swing

Time-to-Therapy Shift

Coverage Shift

Execution-to-Outcome Lag Check

Activity moved; does the demand shift align with expected lag timing (or not)
