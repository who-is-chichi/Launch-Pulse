Design direction (recommended)

Theme: Modern Clinical Ops
Vibe: calm, sharp, high-trust, executive-friendly. Lots of white space, soft surfaces, crisp typography, subtle depth.

Key principles:

Slate neutrals (not pure gray) for a modern look

One strong brand blue for focus + CTAs

Severity colors are muted but clear (no neon)

Cards look like “control panels,” not “tiles”

Typography + layout polish

Typeface: Inter

Add tight tracking for headings (−1% to −2%)

Use tabular numerals for KPIs (monospace-like alignment)

12-column grid, max content width 1200–1280 inside 1440 frame

Spacing: 8pt system (8/16/24/32)

Components styling (to look modern)

Cards: radius 12, border 1px #E2E8F0, shadow:

shadow/sm: 0 1 2 0 rgba(15,23,42,0.06)

shadow/md: 0 8 20 -12 rgba(15,23,42,0.25)

Buttons:

Primary: brand blue fill, white text

Secondary: white surface + border

Tertiary: text button, subtle hover bg

Tables: compact, sticky header, subtle zebra optional (very light: #F8FAFC)

Data viz style (so charts don’t look cheap)

Minimal gridlines (border/subtle)

1 highlight color (brand) + neutrals

Tooltips: dark surface with white text

Sparklines: thin stroke, no heavy fills
